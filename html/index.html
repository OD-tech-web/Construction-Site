Contact Us

Francon Construction ltd is a construction and engineering company located in Nigeria. Specializing in New construction, residential renovations, and commercial construction. Francon ltd always strives towards sustainable, eco-friendly construction and best pratices. The company thrives on making the difficult, delightful and is particularly passionate about Kitchen and bathroom remodeling and room additions.

Our Services

Francon is often recognized as one of the leading general constructing companies in Nigeria due to superior craftsmanship and ability to understand and execute high-level aesthetic excellence that clients desire. whether the project calls for a traditional, contemporary, or modern design.

The team will oversee every phase of construction. These teams are compromised of experts who specialize in numerous types of services and construction projects.

General constructing

From remodeling to building a new home from the ground up, Francon is passionate about creating a rewarding construction experience for our clients, our designers and trade partners, and our team.

Custom Home Building

The team at Francon possess a powerful understanding of the design process, and the creativity to synthesize it with construction planning.

Kitchen Remodeling

Remodeling the kitchen is a great way to give a home a facelift. Working within the client’s budget, Francon provides the best service and return on investment.

Bathroom Remodeling

Francon can transform any bathroom into a desirable retreat ⁠— bringing the vision of the homeowner to life by redesigning and remodeling their old bathrooms and transforming them into a dream space.

Room Additions

Adding additional rooms is an important job and is best left to an experienced room addition contractor. Francon provides renditions of the general and specific areas of the home to present the best plan of action for the new addition.

Commercial Construction

Whether you need to renovate a restaurant or build a new, high-end spa, Francon quality workmanship makes the planning and construction process simple and easy ⁠— resolving in a practical and attractive commercial space.
